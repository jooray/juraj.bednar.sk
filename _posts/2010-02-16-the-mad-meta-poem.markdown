One day,  
 A mad meta-poet,  
 With little to say,  
 Wrote a mad meta-poem  
 That started, "One day...

. . .

. . .

...Sort of close,"  
 Were the words that the poet  
 Finally chose  
 To bring his mad poem  
 To some sort of close.

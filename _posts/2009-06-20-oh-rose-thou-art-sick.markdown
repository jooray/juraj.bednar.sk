Oh Rose, thou art sick!

Oh Rose, thou art sick!  
The invisible worm  
That flies in the night,  
in the howling storm  


  
Has found out thy bed  
Of crimson joy.  
And with his dark secret love,  
does thy life destroy.  


William Blake
